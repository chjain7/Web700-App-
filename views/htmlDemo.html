Examples of Endangered Animals

  • Amur Leopard

    1. Endangered species found in Russia
    2. Only about 100 left in the wild
    3. Hunted for their fur and habitat loss due to logging and development

  • Sumatran Elephant

    1. Endangered species found in Indonesia
    2. Threatened by habitat loss due to deforestation and human encroachment
    3. Only around 2000-3000 left in the wild

  • Vaquita

    1. Critically endangered species of porpoise found in Mexico
    2. Only about 10 left in the wild
    3. Threatened by accidental entanglement in fishing nets

  • Mountain Gorilla

    1. Endangered species found in the forests of central Africa
    2. Only around 1000 left in the wild
    3. Threatened by habitat loss and poaching

  • Sea Otter

    1. Endangered species found along the coast of the northern Pacific Ocean
    2. Threatened by hunting for their fur and oil spills
    3. Play a crucial role in the health of kelp forests by controlling sea urchin populations

This section provides examples of endangered animals and their current situation. It emphasizes the importance of conservation efforts to protect these animals and their habitats from further harm and possible extinction.
